I became a supervisor (Working Lead) in my location about 6 months after I hired into my role. I worked in Tomorrowland until 2017 when I transferred to Main Street. I received a lot of professional growth, and was given opportunities to improve myself and learn new skills.
Data Analyst (2021)

I spent many hours learning on my own, and in October of 2021, I applied for an internal position as a Data Analyst at Disneyland. I was given an hourly position until May 2022 and was then promoted to a salaried role. Here, I have been learning how to build web-apps and have been given the opportunity to continue my education through Disney's Aspire Program.
